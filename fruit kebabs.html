fruit kebabs

prep time: 10 mins

yield: 2

ingredients






steps

  1. Washed, peeled and chopped the fruits into cubes.
  2. Than add salt, black pepper powder,honey, oil and lemon juice.
  3. Mix well and keep aside for 10 minutes.
  4. Now arrange one by one all fruits cube into the toothpick.
  5. Grill these kabab in oven for 2-3 minutes. You can roast on tawa.
  6. Fruit kabab is ready to serve.
