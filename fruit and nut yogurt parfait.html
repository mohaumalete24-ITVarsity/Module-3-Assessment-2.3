FRUIT And NUT YOGURT PARFAIT

prep time: 40min

yield: 2

ingredients









steps

  1. First mix Cinnamon powder and sugar in yogurt nicely.
  2. Now pour yogurt in serving glass and refrigerate for 1/2 an hour.
  3. After 1/2 an hour put watermelon pieces on yogurt.
  4. Now add mango pieces.
  5. At last garnish wid tutti frooti and chopped almonds.
  6. Now rub lemon slice on rim of the glass and rub coconut powder on it.
  7. Serve chilled.
